## üì¶ Batch Download Script

Once you find images you like on Unsplash/Pexels:

1. Right-click image
2. "Save Image As..."
3. Save with proper name:
   - Team: `ravi.jpg`, `priya.jpg`, etc.
   - News: `expansion.jpg`, `funding.jpg`, etc.
   - Blog: `urban-mobility.jpg`, etc.
